How it works

1

Install the Chrome extension

One-click install from the Chrome Web Store. No accounts, no sign-up, no configuration.

2

Join your video call

Works with Google Meet, Zoom Web, and Microsoft Teams Web. Just join your call as usual.

3

Press Alt+D to activate

Or click the AirDraw icon in your toolbar. A small "LIVE" badge confirms it's active.

4

Point and draw

Extend your index finger toward the webcam and move it. Your path becomes ink on your video feed, visible to everyone on the call in real time.

5

Stop, clear, continue

Make a fist to stop drawing. Open your palm to clear. Press Alt+Z to undo. Press Alt+D again to deactivate entirely.

Built with privacy in mind

Everything runs locally in your browser. No server, no cloud, no accounts. Your camera data never leaves your device. Ever.
